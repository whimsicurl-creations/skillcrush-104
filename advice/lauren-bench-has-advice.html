Hi, my name is Lauren and I have some advice for you!

The only thing that will make you feel better is doing the work.

Every day. Even if you can only get 10 minutes of practice in, put in some work as many days as possible.

Sometimes, you'll only have enough brain capacity to review a few videos you've already completed. That's ok! Big transitions happen with small, daily changes in routine.
