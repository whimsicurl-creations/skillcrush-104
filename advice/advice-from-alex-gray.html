How I started out 6 months ago

I started my coding journey on Udemy and W3Schools. This was great, but for me I soon found some of the courses I had started on Udemy had their downsides. I was a complete beginner and they expected me to have some prior knowledge, or I felt I needed a more in depth breakdown of what was going on.

A great way to learn something new outside of Skillcrush!

When I discovered Free Code Camp I felt like I had turned a corner. They have recently updated the entire site and include so many exercises across a vast curicculum! They really break things down into manageable steps with end of section projects bringing everything together with a certificate!
