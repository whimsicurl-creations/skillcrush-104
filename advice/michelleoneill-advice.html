Hi my name is Michelle and I have some advice for you!

Motivation is key!

When you start off at Skillcrush, I'd say it would be common to start off with a hiss and a roar. Until a roadblock hits you like a tonne of bricks. You're stuck, feel stupid and wonder why you're doing this in the first place.

Never fear though. Help is only a mouse-pad click away!

Finding what motivates you helps.

When you're on the journey of coding and web development, try to remember why you started doing this in the first place.

Remember how much of a buzz it was when you completed the 10-day bootcamp? You were thirsty for more and could see a brighter work future. Something that actually excites you!

Some days it would help to have a motivating pod-cast to listen to while you're walking your dog along the beach, or when you first wake up in the morning?
